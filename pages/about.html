Colorado Coed Soccer League (CCSL) was founded in 1987 and is one of Colorado's premier adult recreational soccer leagues.

CCSL is comprised of 16 teams organized into 2 divisions, and we are kept in line by some of Colorado's best certified officials.

We play during the Spring and Fall at Englewood High School.

Our schedule consists of 7 weeks of regular season game play, and a 3 week knock-out style playoff bracket, so 10 weeks in all if a team makes it all the way to the championship.

Players are required to be 21 years of age and up, and represent a wide range of experience levels.

CCSL also sponsors a league celebration at the end of every season.
